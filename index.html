The Standard

Every piece, verified
at the source.

Before a vendor lists on Maison Oré, our provenance team visits the atelier, meets the maker, and documents the process. Nothing enters our catalog without a signed certificate of origin.

The result is a marketplace with the discretion of a private house and the reach of the modern web — where you know exactly who made what you carry home.
